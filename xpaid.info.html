Account Deletion in the "Xpaid" App

This page provides a step-by-step guide on how to request account deletion and related data — in accordance with Google Play requirements.

1. How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Xpaid app and go to Profile. At the bottom you will find a delete button. Follow the instructions.
  2. If you cannot log into your account, send a deletion request to ceo@xpaid.org or fill out the form at: https://example.com/delete-request.
  3. In your request, please include: your email address, username (if any), reason for deletion, and confirmation that you wish to delete your account.

2. What data will be deleted

  • Personal data: name, email, profile fields.
  • Account settings and preferences.

3. What data may be retained

  • Financial and accounting records — as required by law and internal policies (if payments exist).
  • Anonymized technical logs and statistics for security and incident investigation (stored for up to 90 days).

4. Deletion timeline

We will take primary action to delete your data within 7 business days after receiving your request. Complete removal of all backups and related records may take up to 30 days.

Important: if your jurisdiction requires longer retention periods (e.g., for financial data), we reserve the right to retain such records as required by law.

5. How we confirm deletion

After successful deletion, you will receive a notification at the email provided in your request, describing which data was deleted and which was retained, along with the reasons.

6. Contacts

If you have any questions — contact us at: ceo@xpaid.org
